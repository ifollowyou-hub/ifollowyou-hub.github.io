About Me

I'm Daniel, a North Carolina native. I started my IT journey like most - on the Helpdesk. Then, after growing to a Team Lead I was given the opportunity to tackle some problems infinitely more complex than any algorithm - managing people. I've learned so much about what motivates people and how to build cohesive teams.

Currently, I am dedicated to my professional impact by working with teams to build software. There are few things more inspiring to me than truly improving how teams collaborate for a common purpose, and I've found software engineering to be the most powerful way to do it.


In my spare time, you can find me working on or playing my guitars. I also enjoy backpacking and mountain biking.
